Chinese Language Elective Programme · 语特计划

CLEP

悦习社 · Yuè Xí Society · Vice Chair 副主席

The Chinese Language Elective Programme (CLEP) at Chung Cheng High School is a structured enrichment programme for students with strong commitment to Chinese language and culture. Involvement spans the full four secondary school years: beginning as a scholarship recipient, progressing through active production roles, and culminating in the Vice Chair position of the affiliated society, 悦习社.

The range of work produced under CLEP — music films, promotional videos, 3D printing projects, and society events — reflects the programme's emphasis on creative application of language, not just academic proficiency. This page documents that full body of work.

语特不只是一门课，它是一个框架——让语言能力在真实的创作项目和领导角色中得到检验。从奖学金到副主席，四年的投入有始有终。这一页记录的，就是这四年的完整轨迹。

Operational Coordination
副主席 · 负责跨组协调与执行

The Vice Chair role in 悦习社 is primarily an operational one: ensuring that all subcommittees are aligned, that project proposals are tracked and followed up, and that execution meets the standards the society has set for itself. It is not the most visible role, but it is the one that determines whether the society's programmes actually run as planned.

Day-to-day responsibilities include coordinating group leaders, maintaining project timelines, managing attendance records, and troubleshooting logistical issues across events. In practice, this often means identifying problems before they become visible to the broader membership and resolving them without disruption to scheduled activities.

Specific initiatives overseen: the Tanghulu booth, inter-class recruitment drives, orientation for new CLEP members, and the Chinese New Year quiz competition. Each required cross-functional coordination and close attention to execution detail.

副主席的工作大部分是看不见的——问题解决了，活动顺利进行，大家觉得"好像挺顺"，但那不是偶然。

From Lyrics to Screen
从歌词到画面

Produced the music film for the CLEP society song — managing the complete production pipeline from pre-production through to final delivery. Responsibilities included concept development, script writing, principal cinematography, and post-production editing in Adobe Premiere.

The production took place during the secondary three examination period, which required careful time management: filming was scheduled around exam revision, and editing sessions ran late to maintain the delivery timeline. The practical constraint shaped the production approach — every shot needed to be planned in advance, with limited margin for reshoots.

The core creative challenge was translation: the society song's lyrics operate at a level of abstraction that doesn't map directly onto visual imagery. Each scene required a decision about how to render a concept or emotion in a way that felt earned rather than illustrative. That process — working out how language and image can carry the same meaning through different means — was the most significant part of the project.

这个项目最难的不是技术，而是判断：歌词在讲的那些抽象的东西，怎样在画面里找到对应的具体形象，而不显得生硬或刻意？

Production Context

拍摄和剪辑跨越了考试周——复习笔记摊在旁边，时间轴开在屏幕上。这不是最理想的工作条件，但它也是真实的学生创作状态：资源有限，时间有限，但要求不打折。

Production overlapped with the secondary three examination period. Editing sessions ran alongside revision for major papers. The constraint was real, but it also defined the project: this was student creative work made under genuine academic pressure, not a dedicated production block. The final piece reflects what is achievable when the standards are maintained even when the conditions are not ideal.

Produced a promotional video for CLEP society recruitment. The project involved collaborative concept development with the team, followed by independent handling of the full post-production workflow — editing, colour grading, and final export.

🎓
CLEP Scholarship
语特奖学金 · 2024

Awarded the CLEP Scholarship in 2024, recognising sustained commitment to the Chinese Language Elective Programme and demonstrated proficiency in Chinese language across academic and creative contexts.

The scholarship is awarded to students who show not only academic performance in Chinese but consistent engagement with the broader goals of the programme — cultural literacy, creative application, and sustained participation. It is not awarded automatically.

语特奖学金不只看成绩，看的是对整个计划的投入深度。这个奖是对这份投入的认可。

3D Printing Project

Participated in a CLEP project involving 3D printing technology — learning CAD-based design workflows and producing physical objects to support programme activities. The project extended the CLEP framework into design and fabrication, connecting language and culture with hands-on technical work in an unexpected direction.

An example of how CLEP operates across domains: the programme's emphasis on Chinese cultural expression accommodates a wider range of creative and technical formats than standard language instruction.
