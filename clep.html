Vice Chair isn't the person on stage — it's the person who makes sure everyone else gets on stage. Coordinating group leaders, tracking project proposals, enforcing attendance, ensuring execution across every subcommittee.

From Lyrics to Screen
从歌词到画面

The brief: produce a music film for the CLEP society song. The context: it happens during exam week. The outcome: something we're genuinely proud of.

Responsibilities spanned the entire pipeline — pre-production planning, scriptwriting, principal cinematography, and post-production editing. The hardest part wasn't any of those individually. It was translating lyrics that lived in metaphor into visual moments that felt earned, not forced.

考试周。歌词是抽象的，镜头得是具体的。最后剪完的那一刻，屏幕亮着，我知道值了。
