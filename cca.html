The Unglamorous Work
没人看见的那些事

ARTFEST 2024 props core crew from Secondary 2 onwards. Then Quartermaster — the person responsible for all physical resources: storage, inventory, deployment, and the occasional "where did that box go."

Props crew is the job nobody posts about on Instagram. It's also the job that tells you the most about how a production actually works. You learn every set piece, every costume quick-change, every technical constraint, because you were the one who moved it all.

道具管理员不上镜，但没有道具管理员，台上什么都上不了。Sec2就开始干了。
