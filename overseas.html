Overseas Exchange Programmes

Overseas Immersions

海外浸濡 · Three Journeys, One Lens

Each immersion programme presented a distinct context for visual documentation and cultural engagement: Taiwan in 2025 as Memories Committee Leader, Hangzhou in 2026 as the primary videographer, and Chongqing through a student buddy exchange programme. The three experiences, taken together, represent a sustained effort to document and understand Chinese-language cultural environments outside Singapore.

每一趟浸濡都有不同的角色和任务。台湾是带团队，杭州是独立主导视频制作，重庆是作为学伴参与跨文化交流。三段经历，三种视角，同一个目标：记录真实的文化接触。

📷 Memories Committee Leader
Leading the Documentation
回忆组长 · 带队记录整段旅程

As Memories Committee Leader, the primary responsibility was to ensure comprehensive visual documentation of the entire Taiwan immersion — not just capturing individual moments, but building a coherent archive that would allow 30+ participants to revisit the full experience after returning to Singapore.

This involved managing a team of photographers across simultaneous activities, assigning coverage responsibilities, maintaining consistent quality standards across contributors, and making real-time calls when conditions changed. The role sits at the intersection of photography, project management, and editorial judgement.

Documentation scope: still photography across all programme activities, video production for key moments, and coordination with the wider trip committee to ensure coverage gaps were identified and addressed proactively.

这个角色的核心是"对结果负责"——不是我一个人拍好就行，是整个团队的覆盖质量都要达到标准。

Field Challenge: Equipment Failure

行程最密集的一天，主摄相机电池耗尽，备用电池也见底。现场几乎没有补拍机会。当时的处理方式：重新分配团队成员的拍摄任务，优先保证关键活动的覆盖，同时把剩余电量集中到最重要的时段。那次以后，备用电源的数量从未低于两倍预估用量。

On the highest-density shooting day, the primary camera ran out of power mid-session with no practical reshoot window. Response: redistribute team members' assignments to prioritise critical activities, concentrate remaining battery capacity on the most significant segments, and document the contingency plan for future trips. The practical lesson — carry double the batteries you estimate — has held since.

Hangzhou Immersion

Tradition and Modernity, Coexisting
传统与现代并存的城市

Hangzhou presents an unusual visual and cultural context: a city where Song Dynasty gardens, UNESCO World Heritage sites, and the West Lake sit alongside one of China's most prominent technology corridors, drone tourism, and cashless payment infrastructure. That coexistence became the central subject of the video documentation.

Took full responsibility for video production — developing pre-trip shot lists, capturing principal footage across all immersion sites, conducting interviews with programme participants, and producing the final edited documentation. This represented a step up in editorial autonomy from the Taiwan immersion: less coordination, more authorial decision-making about what the documentation should say and how it should be structured.

Locations documented include West Lake, Lingyin Temple, Longjing Tea Plantation, and various sites in the Hangzhou technology district. The edited piece aimed to hold the tension between historical depth and contemporary energy without resolving it artificially.

这次的任务是独立完成整个视频制作流程。从拍摄计划到最终剪辑，都要做出有主张的决定——不只是记录什么发生了，而是如何呈现这个城市的两种面孔。

Editorial Approach

杭州这趟最有趣的挑战不是技术层面，而是立场层面：面对一个传统和现代同等真实存在的城市，视频制作者应该如何处理这种张力？是强调对比，还是呈现共存？最后选择了后者——让两个面向同时出现在画面里，不加评判地并置。

The central editorial question wasn't technical — it was about perspective: when documenting a city where historical depth and contemporary energy are equally present, what is the right authorial stance? The approach taken was to allow both to appear in frame simultaneously, juxtaposed without editorial resolution. The viewer is left to make sense of the relationship. That choice defined the structure of the final piece.

Chongqing Exchange

重庆 · Student Buddy Programme

🤝
Student Buddy Programme
学生伙伴计划

Participated in a cross-cultural exchange programme with visiting students from Chongqing, serving as a local buddy and cultural guide. The experience involved navigating the differences between two distinct Chinese-language educational systems — different pedagogical approaches, different cultural assumptions, and a shared language that nonetheless carries different connotations in each context.

Responsibilities included campus orientation, activity facilitation, and informal cultural exchange across the duration of the visit. The role required attentiveness to when an exchange partner was confused, navigating difference without overexplaining, and finding common ground across genuinely distinct experiences.

同样说中文，但新加坡和重庆的中文教育经历差别很大。做学伴的意义，是在这个差别中找到可以真正交流的地方。

🌆
Cross-cultural Engagement
跨文化体验与反思

The Chongqing exchange complemented the overseas immersion experiences by inverting the dynamic: rather than travelling to document another context, the task was to represent and explain Singapore's context to someone experiencing it for the first time. That reversal offered a different kind of cultural education — learning to articulate what is usually taken for granted.

The experience reinforced an ongoing interest in how Chinese-language culture manifests differently across different societies and educational systems — a theme that runs through the CLEP commitment, the overseas immersions, and the correspondent work.

把"出去看世界"反过来做一次：解释我们自己的环境，而不是记录别人的。这种反转有它独特的学习价值。
