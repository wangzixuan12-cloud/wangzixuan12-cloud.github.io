Overseas Immersions

海外浸濡 · Three countries, one lens

出走是为了看得更清楚。台湾的庙宇、杭州的西湖、镜头后面的眼睛——每一趟浸濡都改变了我看世界的方式。

Leaving Singapore taught me more about seeing than staying ever could. Each immersion changed how I look at culture, memory, and the relationship between old and new.

As Memories Committee Leader, the job was deceptively simple: document the entire trip. The reality was managing a whole team of photographers, coordinating coverage across multiple simultaneous activities, and making sure that 30+ participants would walk away with a record of their experience that did justice to it.

Mid-trip battery failure on the busiest shooting day. Almost no opportunity to reshoot. Lesson learned: carry twice the batteries you think you need, because Murphy was a photographer too.

Hangzhou Immersion
杭州浸濡 · Where tradition and technology coexist without apology
2026
Old City, New Code
传统与现代并存

Hangzhou is a city where you can stand in front of a Song Dynasty garden and pay for your tea via WeChat Pay. That coexistence — unhurried, unapologetic — became the lens through which I documented the entire trip.

Responsible for all video production: planning shot lists, capturing key moments, editing the final documentation. This time, not just an observer — a deliberate visual author.

西湖边，无人机在飞，茶馆里手机扫码点单。传统和现代在这里不是矛盾——它们是同一个城市的两种语言。

Reflection

这趟旅程之后，我开始认真思考：传统和现代的关系，到底是继承，是转化，还是彼此为对方提供意义？镜头对着西湖，脑子里想的是这些。

After Hangzhou, I started asking a question that hasn't resolved itself yet: is the relationship between tradition and modernity one of inheritance, transformation, or mutual meaning-making? I was pointing a camera at West Lake and thinking about this. Make of that what you will.
