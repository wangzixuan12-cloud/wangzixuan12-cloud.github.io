Student · Creator · Leader

考试周的凌晨两点，我还在剪CLEP社歌MV的最后一个镜头。旁边摊着复习笔记，屏幕上是Premiere的时间轴。那一刻我意识到，「学生」和「创作者」不是两个身份——它们是同一回事。

四年来，我用镜头记录、用视频叙事、用文字思考。从台湾浸濡的回忆组长到新闻挑战赛的金奖，从道具间到剪辑台，我一直在做同一件事：把看到的世界，翻译给别人。

2am during exam week, I was still cutting the final shot of the CLEP music film — revision notes spread beside me, Premiere's timeline glowing on screen. That's when I understood: being a student and being a creator aren't two different things. They never were.

Four years. One lens. One through-line: translating the world I see into something others can feel.
