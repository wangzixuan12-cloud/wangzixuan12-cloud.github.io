📷 Student Photojournalist
Shooting for a Newspaper
为报纸按下快门

As a Lianhe Zaobao student photojournalist, the job was to document school and community events with journalistic intent — not just capturing what happened, but capturing what mattered about what happened.

The training covered news values, visual storytelling, caption writing, and editorial standards. A systematic education in the difference between a record and a story.

为联合早报拍照，不是在拍漂亮图片，是在拍新闻。两者的分别很微妙，但真实存在。

Awarded Best Group Photo at the 38th Zaobao Correspondents awards. A group photo isn't just everyone standing in a row — it's arranging people, reading dynamics, timing the shutter to the moment when the group is most itself.

What Journalism Taught Me About Photography

一张照片如果没有新闻价值，它再好看也只是装饰。通讯员经历让我开始问：这张照片在讲什么？不是"看起来怎样"，是"它说了什么"。

A beautiful photograph with no news value is wallpaper. The correspondent training forced me to ask: what is this photo saying? Not "does it look good" — "does it mean something." That question changed the way I shoot everything.
