2023 · Sec 1
The Science Detour
理科发展计划 · 国大实验室

The Science Development Programme in Secondary 1 placed me in NUS research lab environments early — learning experimental methodology, scientific thinking, and what it means to actually investigate something rather than just answer questions about it.

I ended up in the humanities and arts. But the SDP taught me something that survived the detour: how to ask better questions.

中一就进了国大实验室。后来往文艺方向走了，但那段科学思维的训练一直都在。学会问更好的问题。

Everything Accelerates

CNY Hampers Project
新年礼篮项目 · 主要组织者

Led the organisation of Chinese New Year hamper deliveries for community members. The logistics of care — sourcing, packing, coordinating delivery, and making sure the person at the door felt the intention behind the box.

礼篮不只是礼篮，是有人记得你。组织这件事让我明白了这一点。

Guided Primary 2 students through reading as part of a school-community programme. Teaching reading is mostly about patience — and figuring out that every struggling reader has a different reason for struggling.
